<h2 id="our-mission">Our mission</h2><p>LeftHanded.io exists for the 10% of people who reach with the wrong hand by default. Most products and tools are designed for the other 90%. We try to fix that — with practical guides, honest product reviews, and the kind of detail you only notice when you've been a lefty long enough to stop being surprised by it.</p><h2 id="what-we-offer">What we offer</h2><p>Tips, reviews, and reference articles for daily life on the left side. Tools that actually work, instructions written in lefty orientation, and explanations of why certain things feel awkward (and how to make them feel less awkward). He's a 41-year-old lefty based in the Southeast US — not native to the region, but he stayed. He's spent enough years working around right-handed defaults to have opinions about most of them, and this site is where the opinions land.</p><p>Lefty or not, thanks for stopping by.</p>
