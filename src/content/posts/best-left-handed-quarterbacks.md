<p>The NFL has been full of some great quarterbacks, but it seems that more often than not they are right-handed. Despite guiding the NFL's Denver Broncos to the playoffs in 2011, the popular quarterback was transferred to the New York Jets and only stayed in the league for one more season. He was a member of the Tampa Bay Buccaneers as well.</p><h2 id="ken-stabler">Ken Stabler</h2><figure class="kg-card kg-image-card kg-card-hascaption"><img src="/content/images/2022/02/ken-stabler.jpg" class="kg-image" alt="" loading="lazy" width="500" height="552"><figcaption>Ken Stabler</figcaption></figure><p>Ken Stabler spent 17 seasons in the NFL, largely with the Oakland Raiders.
