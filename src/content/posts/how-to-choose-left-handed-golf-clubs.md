Left-handed players must choose a shaft flex that matches their swing speed and tempo. If the shaft is too stiff, it can result in less distance and accuracy. On the other hand, if the grip is too large, it can cause tension in your hands and wrists, leading to inconsistency in your shots.</p><p>Consider hand size, grip pressure, and personal preference to determine the correct grip size.
